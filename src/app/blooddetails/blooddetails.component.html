Centralized Blood Transfusion Management System

A lightweight desktop application designed to modernize and streamline the workflow of blood transfusion centers. No more scattered records or manual tracking — this C-based solution centralizes donor data, manages blood transfers, and ensures smooth coordination between donation sites and hospitals.

Engineered for operational reliability and ease of use, it empowers medical administrators with efficient tools to register donors, monitor inventories, and track transfusion activities in real time.

  • C-Based Desktop Solution

  • Built for Medical Administrators

  • Focused on Security & Data Integrity
